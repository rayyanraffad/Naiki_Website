# 01_PRD.md — Naiki Website Product Requirements Document (AI Optimized)

# Project Objective

Create an award-winning editorial website for **Naiki** that builds trust, inspires action, and converts visitors into donors, volunteers, and partners.

The website should communicate Naiki's mission through storytelling rather than traditional NGO layouts.

Mission:

**Breaking barriers with education & outreach. Empowering youth with voice, leadership and skills.**

---

# Primary Goals

1. Build trust immediately.
2. Tell Naiki's story emotionally.
3. Showcase measurable impact.
4. Increase volunteer registrations.
5. Increase donations.
6. Position Naiki as a modern, credible nonprofit.

---

# Target Audience

* Donors
* Volunteers
* Youth
* Educational institutions
* NGOs
* Corporate partners
* Sponsors

---

# Homepage Story Flow

Visitors should experience the website in this order:

1. Emotional Hero
2. Why Naiki Exists
3. The Problem
4. Our Solution
5. Programs
6. Community Impact
7. Success Stories
8. Gallery
9. Get Involved
10. Donate
11. Contact

Never change this order without a compelling UX reason.

---

Navigation

* Sticky

Hero

* Full viewport

Programs

Impact

Stories

* Carousel

Gallery

Donation

* Trust-first layout

Footer

* Newsletter
* Social links
* Contact
* Copyright

# Quality Gate

The website should never resemble:

* Bootstrap
* Generic NGO templates
* SaaS landing pages
* Startup templates

Every viewport should feel individually designed.

Every section should contribute to a cohesive editorial narrative.
